Many students study English for years. They know the rules, the words, and the grammar. But when it is time to speak, they freeze.

Not because they are lazy. Not because they are unintelligent.

Because most English education trains recognition, not real-time speaking.

Star Speaker was created to solve that gap.

You freeze when it is time to speak.

You need a system that makes speaking unavoidable — but safe.
